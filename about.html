The Leave Application System is a comprehensive digital solution designed specifically for the Provincial Information Technology Office (PITO) of the Provincial Government of Bulacan. This system modernizes the traditional paper-based leave application process, making it more efficient, accessible, and environmentally friendly.

Built with simplicity and functionality in mind, our system ensures that employees can easily file their leave applications while maintaining compliance with Civil Service Form No. 6 (Revised 2020) standards.
